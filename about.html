About Wekka

Recruiters can't find talent. When they try, often they get swamped with loads of irrelevant CVs. Recruitment is a serious pain in the neck.

Job seekers spend ages finding decent jobs to apply to, and then need to apply to tens of them to get a single response. Finding, contacting and applying to recruiters is a nightmare.

Enter Wekka.

Wekka is an online recruitment platform, aiming to change the lives of both Job Seekers and Employers. Recruitment will never be the same.
